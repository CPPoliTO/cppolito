SWERC 2023 selections

Next South-Western European Regional Contest will take place from 17th to 19th of February and, also for this year, we decided to create some selection contests, so that we'll chose the best teams to represent Politecnico di Torino. But, this year, we opted to let partecipate teams from the beginning of the selection contests. Each team can have up to three components (like ICPC rules).

How will it work?

The selection will be made up of two rounds, the first will take place on Sunday, October 16th, starting at 15:00 and it will last 2 and a half hours, the second on Sunday, October 30th. Both contests will be on our Codeforces group. In order to partecipate, you must create a Codeforces account and, then, a team together with your teammates. At this point, you will have to register to the contest (registrations open six hours before the beginning of the contest itself). You must register as a team, not as individual partecipants. Recall to check, before subscribing, if you are elegible following the ICPC regulation.

Moreover, to sign up to the selection, you have to submit this Google form. It is possible to partecipate in teams of one, two or three people.

The final result will be given by the results of both rounds, taking into consideration the sum of the score of the single contests. The three teams with the highest score will represent Politecnico di Torino at SWERC of February 2023.

For any doubt, you can write on our Telegram group.
