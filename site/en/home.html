CPPoliTO Training Contests

CPPoliTO training contests are competitive programming ICPC-style training competitions open for all students from Politecnico di Torino. If you're willing to test your coding and mathematical problem solving skills against other students, or simply train your competitive programming abilities and learn something new, then this contests are definitely for you!

What is competitive programming?

Competitive programming is a mind sport which consists in solving logic and mathematical problems implementing severals algorithms in given programming languages. Learn more.

As University students, the most important competition in which we can partecipate is the International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC). Other than that, there are lots of competitive programming events during the year: Google Code Jam, Google Hashcode, Codeforces Rounds (on a weekly base), and many others...
