CPPoliTO Training Contests

CPPoliTO training contests are competitive programming ICPC-style training competitions open for all students from Politecnico di Torino.
If you're willing to test your coding and mathematical problem solving skills against other students, or simply train your competitive programming abilities and learn something new, then this contests are definitely for you!

Intructions and rules:

You can participate individually or as a team of 2/3 students. The contests will be one week long and the leaderboard is determined by the number of solved problems. In case of ties, the team with the least penalty time has the advantage. Penalty time is the sum of times required to solve each problem plus an additional penalty for each wrong submission.

To register, create an account on Codeforces. In your profile, you can create a team (if you want to participate as a team) in the "TEAMS" section. Then, go to this link and ask to join as participant.

The first contest has been held from Friday 17 December (11:00) to Friday 24 December. At this link you can find the solutions for the A to I problems. For the problem J the solution is to compute Fibonacci numbers until f(i) is <= 1000, then for each f(i) set the character in the string accordingly. Complexity O(n).


For any doubts, visit the Telegram Group

If you're just getting started with competitive programming, this free pdf book could help with the basics.
